On one hand, the area of the shape ABDFH equals AC² + BC² + Area(ΔABC + ΔFMC + ΔFLC). On the other hand, Area(ABDFH) = AB² + Area(ΔBED + ΔFGE + ΔAGH).

Thâbit ibn Qurra's admits a natural generalization to a proof of the Law of Cosines .

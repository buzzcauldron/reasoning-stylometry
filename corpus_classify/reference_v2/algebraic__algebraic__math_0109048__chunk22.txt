We now use our block triangulations to build a model manifold N ∼ = S × I. We build N in stages corresponding to standard triangulations Tj suited to pants decompositions Pj that intervene between PX and PY respectively. At each stage there is a model Nj also homeomorphic to S × I so that the top boundary component ∂ +Nj = S × { 1} is triangulated by the jth triangulation Tj in the sequence of triangulations. Each Nj will be obtained from Nj− 1 by attaching a triangulated block to ∂ +Nj− 1 that realizes the elementary move needed to move from Tj− 1 to Tj.
